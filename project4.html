Exquisite_Corpse

In this project, I combined elements from my own drawing and inspirations from Ruby's and Gavin's artworks to create a unique character. Starting with the head of a previous drawing, I used Ruby's illustration for the upper body and Gavin's for the lower body, modifying details to align with my vision. The girl's angelic resemblance inspired me to omit her feet and change her clothing to pure white, symbolizing innocence. This project taught me how blending styles and adding subtle interactivity can create a deeper narrative.
