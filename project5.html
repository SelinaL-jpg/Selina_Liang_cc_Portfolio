Optical_Illusion

In this project, I explored how simple shapes and animations can create dynamic visual effects inspired by a classic optical illusion. By adjusting the size of the ellipses based on sine waves, I aimed to create a pulsating effect that mimics the movement often seen in visual illusion designs. The use of color shifts tied to the size of the shapes added an additional layer of depth, enhancing the illusion of motion and transformation. The reference to classic optical illusion patterns allowed me to experiment with perception and how subtle changes can lead to unexpected visual outcomes.
